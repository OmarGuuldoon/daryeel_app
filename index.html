Privacy Policy for Daryeel Deegaan

Effective Date: 8th October 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Daryeel Deegaan, a mobile application designed to assist with liquid waste management and collection services. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our app. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled responsibly.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you register or use our services, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, and other contact details.
  • Business Information: For business users, we may collect business names, addresses, and related details necessary for service provision.
  • Usage Data: We collect information on how the app is used, including interaction with features, time spent on the app, and device information (e.g., OS version, device type).
  • Location Data: With your permission, we may collect location data to facilitate service delivery and route optimization.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and improve our services, including scheduling collections and managing payments.
  • Communicate with you regarding service updates, support, or promotional offers (with your consent).
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance app functionality and user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect against fraudulent activities.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data under the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party providers who assist in operating our app (e.g., payment processors, cloud storage), under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices and Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information by contacting us.
  • Opt-out of promotional communications at any time.
  • Disable location tracking through your device settings (note that this may affect certain app functionalities).

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such data, we will take steps to delete it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in legal requirements or our data practices. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Your continued use of the app after such updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
